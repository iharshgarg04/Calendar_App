Introduction to the Calendar

The calendar is a powerful tool to help you organize your time and keep track of important events, tasks, and appointments. It provides a visual representation of dates, typically organized by months, which allows you to plan and manage your schedule effectively.

Key Features

Navigating the Calendar

Change months and return to today with easy-to-use navigation buttons.

Viewing Dates

Quickly identify days of the week, the current month, and year for seamless planning.

Adding Events and Tasks

Add events and tasks with a single click. Edit or view existing events and tasks by clicking on a date.
